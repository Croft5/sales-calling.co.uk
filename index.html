About

My Story

I'm Dave Edmonds, been in sales for over 40 years, most of that time using the telephone, well only that and post back in the 80's & 90's.

The phone is still a powerful and vital way for you to grow your business. What I offer is a unique one to one coaching and training for your staff who need to be effective on the phone for you

Method

The three main steps are firstly to see if you have the right people; it's a demanding role, not right for many.

Then to provide them with the tools and techniques.

Finally, and very importantly, to gift them the personal skills necessary to become great at prospecting and selling over the phone.

Costs

Following an initial meeting, phone, video or in person, there would be an on-site one day session costing £250 so I can get to know your business, staff and processes.

Once we have discussed my report you can decide how many one day sessions would be best; I find that one day a month for say six months is a good staring point.

Each day I will come on-site and spend the day with one or two of your callers, typically 10am to 4pm. Each day is £250; it's important that they have time between sessions to practice their new skills.
